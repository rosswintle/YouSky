About YouSky

YouSky is a personal, private journal that works like social media.

Capture your thoughts in short social-media length posts that only you get to see.

YouSky stores the data in your web browser. There is no "cloud" or "server". Only you, in this web broswer, on this device, can see what you post. (Though you use entirely at your own risk and I'm not responsible for what you do with it.)

You can (and it is recommended that you do) export your posts using the menu in the top-right occasionally to save them. This also allows you to move your posts to another device.

YouSky has replies and hashtags and search that work mostly as you would expect. It's not finished and lacks some polish, but it works just fine today. Expect it to work a bit better in the future.
